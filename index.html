Desk of Hope International Ministries is a non-profit organization founded in March of 2020 by Muramira Denis (Bwengye). DHM aims to restore Hope in the community.
We serve and empower young men and women through Christian outreach, community development, and youth empowerment.
To restore hope among Vulnerable children and communities that have been affected by poverty, floods, wars and abuse.
Communities where children and their care-givers are empowered to improve their livelihood through appropriate and affordable alternatives.
  • Empowerment through Community Development.
  • Honesty and integrity in decision making.
  • Passionate to see lives Radically Transformed for Christ.
  • Giving back to the community and being good stewards with what we are given.
  • Restoring Hope in the community.

What We Do.

We believe that we can save more lifes with you.

Healthy Food

We address nutritional challenges in vulnerable communities, offering wholesome meals to restore physical and spiritual well-being. Join us in giving back, fostering good stewardship, and bringing hope through nourishment.

Pure Water

Desk of Hope provides clean water to vulnerable communities, aligning with our values of empowerment and community development. Join us in restoring hope through life-sustaining hydration.

Health Care

We deliver essential healthcare to vulnerable communities, embodying our values of empowerment and community development. Join us in restoring hope through accessible and compassionate healthcare services.

Primary Education

We champion Primary Education, aligning with our values of empowerment and community development. Join us in restoring hope through accessible and quality education for vulnerable communities.

Residence Facilities

We provide safe Residence Facilities, aligning with our values of empowerment and community development. Join us in restoring hope through secure and supportive living environments for vulnerable communities.

Social Care

We extend Social Care initiatives, exemplifying our commitment to empowerment and community development. Join us in restoring hope through compassionate support for vulnerable individuals and communities.
